About Us

Mario Kemper, developer and project founder

I am a computer science student from Cologne, Germany. As a side job, I work in quality assurance for a local software company. This is were I’ve developed the idea of Shutter (You might want to have a look at my interview at http://blog.launchpad.net to read more about the reasons and goals of the project: http://blog.launchpad.net/projects/shutter).

I am addicted to free software and community driven projects. In addition to modest abilities in C/C++, PHP and some experiences with .NET languages I am mostly interested in Perl (Shutter is written in Perl as well) nowadays.

Vadim Peretokin, promotion, web presence and interface design

I’m an IT healthcare student in YorkU, Toronto. I’m interested in promoting quality open-source software and my work in Shutter includes expanding it’s popularity and interface design. Programming languages of interest are C and Vala.
